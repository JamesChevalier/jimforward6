I'm Jim Chevalier, and I want to be your City Councilor. I grew up on Northampton Street in Ward 6, graduated from the Holyoke Public School system, and studied at the University of Vermont.

I've lived in Ward 6 for most of my life. My wife, Noel, and I purchased our home here three years ago. We have been working hard to make improvements and repairs to our home, and have carried that drive into making our community a better place as well. As a founding member of c.r.u.s.h. (Citizens for the Revitalization and Urban Success of Holyoke), I have worked to create community events large and small which bring together Holyokers. Our monthly Party with a Purpose draws speakers on topics like arts in Holyoke and energy conservation, and opens dialogue about issues facing our city. I want to harness this energy, the energy of our voices, and create a comprehensive approach to representing Ward 6.

As a web developer, I work every day to take client's ideas out of their heads an onto a web page. In order to do that, I have to be able to understand what they're requesting, and accurately explain my thoughts, ideas, and plans for their future. This requires a great ability to not only hear what people are saying, but to understand their greater ideas and how they relate to the world at large. I must set a successful plan into motion and keep my clients updated along the way. As your representative in City Council, I will use these same strategies to create transparent, responsive and successful leadership.

I want to make sure that residents are able to be heard by their representative. I think that a leader who celebrates our diversity, understands our challenges, and strives to improve quality of life for all residents is what we need and I want to be that person.
